We're hiring a postdoc

August 31, 2019 Jingjie Yeo

The J2 Lab for Engineering Living Materials (jingjieyeo.github.io) in the Sibley School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Cornell University invites applications for one Postdoctoral Research position, slated to begin in January 2020. The Postdoctoral Researcher will spearhead integrated, interdisciplinary development and application of multiscale computational modeling techniques, in conjunction with artificial intelligence/machine learning, in the following areas:

• Synthetic biology pertaining to the human microbiome
 • Metallic alloys and ceramics for 3D printing/additive manufacturing
 • Dynamically-responsive and/or functional polymeric or biopolymeric materials
Ideal candidates must show strong commitment to collaborative, communicative, and cross-disciplinary research, as the researcher will work collaboratively with multiple research groups globally. Candidates who demonstrate past achievements in fostering diversity and inclusion in STEM mentorship, education, and research is also strongly preferred. The lab’s principle investigator, Jingjie Yeo, will fully assist the researcher in developing a tailored career development plan for advancing his/her professional goals.

Applicants should have a Ph.D. in any areas of science and engineering related to, but not limited to, biomedicine, materials, biology, chemistry, physics, and mechanics. Ideal candidates should be very proficient in (1) computational simulation techniques (e.g. AI/machine learning, molecular dynamics simulations, flux balance analysis, agent-based modeling) and (2) programming (e.g. Python, R, Julia). Experience with high-performance computing clusters is also desirable. The candidate must demonstrate potential for excellence in research, possess good English verbal and written communication skills, and be able to develop new projects independently in a multidisciplinary research environment.

Candidates are requested to email a cover letter, curriculum vitae, description of research interests (no more than 5 pages in size 12, sans serif fonts), and contact information or recommendation letters of three professional references to jingjieyeo@cornell.edu. Review of applications will start immediately and continue until the position is filled. For any enquiries, please contact Assistant Professor Jingjie Yeo at jingjieyeo@cornell.edu, or visit the lab website at jingjieyeo.github.io.
