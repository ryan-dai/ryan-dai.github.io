Rui Dai

I am a Ph.D. student majoring Mechanical Engineering at School for Engineering of Matter, Transport & Energy, ASU . Before joining ASU, I received my B. S. in Theoretical and Applied Mechanics at University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). I worked as an undergraduate research assistant in Professor Hengan Wu's group from 2010 to 2014. My research interests are Solid Mechanics, Nanomechanics, Material Design, Thermal Mechanics and Manufacturing.
